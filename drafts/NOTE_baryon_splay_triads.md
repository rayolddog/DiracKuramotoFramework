## 1. Two neutralization geometries (solid physics, sketch framing)

In clock/phasor language, nature neutralizes charge in exactly two ways:

- **Anti-phase pair (π-locking).** Two phasors at 180°: particle–antiparticle.
  Electron–positron; and within QCD, the **meson** (color + anticolor). The
  cancellation can *complete*: the pair can annihilate — positronium in
  nanoseconds, π⁰ in 10⁻¹⁷ s. π-locking is self-annihilating.
- **Splay triad (2π/3-locking).** Three phasors at mutual 120° summing to
  zero: the **baryon** (three colors → "white"). No two members are opposites,
  so pairwise annihilation is unavailable — the triad is stuck with itself.
  2π/3-locking is self-protecting: the proton (lifetime > 10³⁴ yr) is the most
  stable composite object known.

Same constituents, same force; the lifetime contrast tracks the phasor
geometry. **Confinement, in this heuristic:** a lone color phasor cannot
cancel by itself, so it cannot exist unaccompanied; a lone electron is fine
because its canceling partner is a *different particle* it simply never met.

**Why no "asymmetry of existence" among colors (solid):** matter–antimatter
asymmetry required a *breakable* symmetry (C/CP, global, violated) plus a
nonequilibrium history (Sakharov conditions). Color SU(3) is *exact and
gauged* — "more red than blue" is not even definable, and confinement enforces
one-of-each locally. Asymmetric existence is the fossil of a breakable mirror;
colors never had one. (Contrast: antiquarks are exactly as suppressed as
positrons; heavier flavors/generations are suppressed too, but by decay —
thermodynamics, not a broken mirror.)

## 2. Masking, and where the mask slips (solid)

Color-singlet combinations always carry **integer** electric charge — the
fractional thirds (u = +2/3, d = −1/3) mask to whole numbers in every allowed
hadron. The masking is incomplete in exactly the ways that prove the hidden
structure:

| Masked quantity | The slip | What it reveals |
|---|---|---|
| neutron charge (0) | magnetic moment −1.91 μ_N | charged phasors with nonzero current distribution |
| proton spin (½) | valence quark spins supply only ~30% ("spin crisis") | gluon spin + orbital motion carry the rest |
| color (white) | form factors, deep inelastic scattering | the six clocks underneath |

MR-native analogy: zero net magnetization with nonzero internal spin
structure. (Pleasing loop-closure: the neutron's magnetic moment — the mask's
most famous slip — is what the Allmendinger comagnetometer bound
b_n < 6.7×10⁻³⁴ GeV rides on, i.e., the anchor constraint of the T-test
program.)

## 3. The anomaly arithmetic: pair and trilogy are conscripted partners (solid)

The rigorous sense in which the "chiral trilogy" is real: **gauge anomaly
cancellation**. Chiral fermions in loops generate anomalies that would destroy
the theory's consistency; per generation they cancel only via

$$3\cdot\tfrac{2}{3} \;+\; 3\cdot\!\left(-\tfrac{1}{3}\right) \;+\; (-1) \;+\; 0 \;=\; 0$$

(colors × up-charge + colors × down-charge + electron + neutrino). The
cancellation works **only for exactly three colors with exactly those
fractional charges**. So the lepton chiral pair and the color-tripled quark
sector are not independent inhabitants of a generation — neither is consistent
without the other, and charge quantization is the visible face of the
conscription.

## 4. The architecture: three chiral pairs, two coupling layers (solid summary)

J.B.'s final formulation is the correct one — not a chiral trilogy (chirality
is exhausted by L/R) but **three chiral pairs**, with the trilogy living in
color space:

- **Within each quark — a chiral pair**: L and R Weyl clocks coupled by the
  chiral condensate (constituent mass ~300 MeV; the QCD clockmaker, vs. the
  Higgs clockmaker of the leptons — see the clockmaker table in the 2026-07-05
  session / `NOTE_cut_crossover_higgs.md` lineage). *The pair gives the tick.*
- **Between the quarks — the splay triad**: the three pairs color-locked at
  120° by gluon exchange. *The triad gives permission to persist.*

Six Weyl clocks; two bonds with two jobs. A lone colored pair can tick but
cannot exist unaccompanied; three uncoupled chiralities could be neutral but
massless.

**No scale separation (solid, and important):** the intra-pair clock
(~300 MeV) and the inter-pair coupling (~Λ_QCD ≈ 200 MeV) run at comparable
rates — unlike the electron in an atom (Compton ≫ orbital). In sync
vocabulary: natural frequencies ~ coupling strengths ⇒ the deeply nonlinear
regime where nothing factorizes. This *is* strong coupling; it is why the
nucleon defeats perturbation theory and requires the lattice. The nucleon is
not three clocks in a box; it is three clocks whose mutual coupling is as loud
as their ticking.

**Valence caveat:** "three pairs" is the valence skeleton (carrier of the
conserved quantum numbers); the physical nucleon adds a sea of q–q̄ pairs and
gluons on top.

## 6. Quarantine flags

This note's geometry is an **analogy with suggestive structure, not a
derivation**. Real color SU(3) is non-Abelian: gluons themselves carry color,
the singlet condition is representation theory rather than a literal phasor
sum, and three phase labels do not capture the group. The anomaly arithmetic
(§3) and the masking facts (§2) are textbook-solid; the splay-locking reading
of them (§1, §5) is the speculative contribution. Keep out of the main paper;
QCD threads live with the Polyakov–Kuramoto note.

## Glossary extensions

- **Splay state** — N oscillators locked at mutual 2π/N offsets; phasor sum
  zero. Three-phase electrical power is the N=3 engineering instance.
- **Meson / baryon** — quark–antiquark pair (π-geometry) / three-quark triad
  (2π/3-geometry); the two color-neutral hadron families.
- **Sakharov conditions** — the three requirements for generating a
  matter–antimatter asymmetry: baryon-number violation, C/CP violation,
  departure from equilibrium.
- **Gauge anomaly** — a quantum (loop-level) violation of a gauge symmetry;
  fatal to consistency unless contributions cancel across the fermion content.
- **Constituent vs current quark mass** — condensate-dressed (~300 MeV)
  vs Higgs-given (few MeV) quark mass.
- **Valence quarks / sea** — the three bookkeeping quarks carrying a baryon's
  quantum numbers / the transient q–q̄ + gluon population beneath them.
- **Proton spin crisis** — the measured fact that valence-quark spins account
  for only ~30% of the proton's spin.
